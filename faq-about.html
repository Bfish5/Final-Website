Frequently asked questions

Questions small business owners ask us most.

If you’re not technical, that’s completely okay. Our job is to keep things simple and transparent so you always know what’s happening and why.

Who is Finesse Web Solutions best for? +
We’re ideal for small, service-based businesses across the United States — plumbers, barbers, contractors, cleaners, electricians, landscapers, handymen, local shops, and more. If your customers search online before they call you, a simple, professional 3-page website can make a big difference.
What exactly do I get for $1,000? +
You get a custom 3-page website, designed around your business, plus free hosting forever. There are no monthly website or hosting fees. The design is modern, mobile-friendly, and built in a calming blue-and-white style to help you look professional and trustworthy online.
Are there any hidden or monthly fees? +
No hidden fees. The website itself is a one-time price: $1,000 with free hosting forever. The only ongoing cost is your domain (like yourbusiness.com), which you’ll buy from GoDaddy or a similar provider. Domain names typically renew yearly with the domain company.
How does the domain work? +
You’ll purchase your domain from GoDaddy or a similar provider so you fully own it. Once you’ve bought it, we connect the domain to your new website for you. You don’t need to worry about DNS settings or technical steps — we handle that part.
How long does it really take? +
Our process is designed to be fast and stress-free. Websites are typically completed in 1–2 days:

– Day 0: Intro call (5–10 minutes).
– Day 1: We build your full demo website.
– Day 1 evening: Zoom session to finalize live with you.
– Day 2: Your website launches once you approve the final design.
What if I don’t like the first version? +
That’s completely okay. The demo is meant to be a starting point. We walk through it with you on Zoom and adjust the wording, images, sections, and layout live until you feel it truly represents your business. We do not launch the website until the client loves the final design.
Do I need to write all of the text for the site? +
No. You just explain your services and what makes your business unique in plain language. We then turn that into clear, simple website copy written for non-technical visitors. If you’d like to adjust any wording, we’ll update it together during the Zoom session.
What happens after the website is launched? +
Once your site is live, it will continue running with free hosting. If you ever need changes or updates, you can reach out and we’ll discuss exactly what you’d like adjusted. Our goal is that your site continues to reflect your current services and brand.
Is this only for businesses in one city? +
We work with small businesses across the United States. Whether you’re in a big city or a smaller town, your website can still present you as professional, organized, and ready for new clients.
What if I don’t currently have any website? +
That’s perfectly fine — in fact, it’s one of the main problems we solve. If you have no online presence, we’ll guide you from nothing to a complete, modern website that gives your business a real online home.
What if my existing website is outdated or broken? +
That’s the other main situation we see. Maybe your site looks old, doesn’t work well on phones, or feels messy and confusing. We’ll create a brand new, clean blue-and-white design that feels fresh, trustworthy, and much easier for customers to use.

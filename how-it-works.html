Detailed timeline

What happens from the first call to launch.

Here’s your full, vertical timeline — from feeling stressed about your current website (or lack of one) to feeling relieved as your new, calming blue-and-white site goes live.

Day 0 — Intro Call
A quick, friendly conversation.

You call or text 443-797-2249. We hop on a 5–10 minute call where you simply explain: what you do, where you serve, and what’s currently frustrating you about your online presence.

You’ll feel heard — not judged. Our goal is to understand your business, not to sell you on complicated extras.

Day 1 Morning — We Build Your Demo
Your new site takes shape behind the scenes.

We take everything you shared and start building your custom 3-page website in our clean blue-and-white style. We organize your services, contact info, and story into a layout that’s easy to skim.

While you go about your day, you’ll know real progress is happening — without you having to touch any tech.

Day 1 Afternoon — Your Demo Is Ready
You see your future website for the first time.

By the next day, we prepare a full demo website with calming blues, navy + sky blue accents, and modern blue gradients that make your brand feel current and trustworthy.

Most clients feel a wave of relief here — “Finally, this is what my business should have looked like online all along.”

Day 1 Evening — Zoom Edit Session
We refine everything live, together.

We get on a Zoom call and walk through each page with you — Home, Services, and Contact (or whichever 3 pages fit your business best). You tell us what you like and what you’d like changed. We adjust it live.

You’ll feel in control and supported. No guessing, no back-and-forth emails, just real-time changes until it feels right.

Day 2 — Launch Day (Your Site Goes Live)
Your new online presence is officially open for business.

Once you’re happy, we connect your domain (purchased from GoDaddy or a similar provider) and launch your site. Usually within minutes, people searching your business will see your new, professional website.

This is the moment your stress turns into confidence. Instead of worrying about your website, you’ll know it’s working quietly in the background to bring you more clients.

And remember: we do not launch the website until you love the final design.
